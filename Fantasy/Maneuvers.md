## Distracting Strike

**Specializations:** Finesse, Martial, Unarmed

**Stamina Cost:** 2

**Action Speed:** Standard

**Resist:** Willpower

Make a melee attack with +1 to hit. If successful, the target must lose concentration on a spell.
